HOW IT WORKS - REFERRALS

1. Get A Referral Code

Upon completing your Buyback Boss Transaction, you will be presented with a unique referral code to share with friends.

2. Share Your Referral Code

If you give someone your referral code and refer them to sell their phone to Buyback Boss, you will receive $10 from us, and they will receive $10 in addition to the amount they receive for their phone.

3. Get Paid!

Once somebody utilizes your referral code, we will contact you informing you that you will be paid, and present you with the different options for receiving your money.
